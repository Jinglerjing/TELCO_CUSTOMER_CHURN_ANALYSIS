**customer personas** 
---

## Persona 1: “At-Risk Price-Sensitive Newcomer” 🔴

**Profile**

* Month-to-month contract
* Short tenure
* High monthly charges
* Fiber optic internet
* Electronic check payment
* No security / support add-ons

**Why they churn**

* Low commitment
* Price shock early in lifecycle
* High expectations from premium service

**Actions**

* 🎯 **Early-life intervention (first 30–90 days)**
* 💰 Offer **temporary discount** or bill credit
* 📦 Bundle **online security + tech support** at reduced price
* 📄 Incentivize switch to **1-year contract**
* 💳 Push **automatic payment** setup

**Expected impact**: High
👉 This persona contributes the **largest churn volume**

---

## Persona 2: “Unprotected Digital User” 🟠

**Profile**

* Has internet service
* No online security, backup, or device protection
* Often month-to-month
* Medium tenure

**Why they churn**

* Low perceived switching cost
* Weak service attachment

**Actions**

* 📦 **Bundle protection services** (security + backup)
* 🧠 Educate on value (“protect your data & devices”)
* 🎁 Free trial of add-ons for 3 months
* 📈 Upsell tied to loyalty rewards

**Expected impact**: Medium–High
👉 Add-ons are strong **churn dampeners**

---

## Persona 3: “Transaction-Based Payer” 🟠

**Profile**

* Pays via **electronic check**
* No automatic payment
* Medium–high churn risk regardless of price

**Why they churn**

* Lower engagement
* Higher payment friction

**Actions**

* 💳 Incentivize **autopay enrollment**

  * Small monthly discount
  * One-time bill credit
* 📲 In-app reminders highlighting convenience
* 🔁 Auto-migration campaigns

**Expected impact**: Medium
👉 Easy operational win, scalable

---

## Persona 4: “Loyal Long-Term Customer” 🟢

**Profile**

* Long tenure
* One-year or two-year contract
* Has add-on services
* Automatic payment
* Often has partner/dependents

**Why they stay**

* High switching cost
* Strong service embedding

**Actions**

* 🎁 **Retention rewards** (not discounts)
* 🔄 Early renewal offers
* 📣 Referral programs
* 🧪 Beta access / loyalty perks

**Expected impact**: Low churn, high LTV
👉 Protect and monetize, don’t discount

---

## Persona 5: “Low-Usage Minimalist” 🟢

**Profile**

* No internet service or DSL
* Low monthly charges
* Stable churn behavior

**Why they stay**

* Simple needs
* Low expectations

**Actions**

* ❌ Avoid aggressive upsell
* 📦 Offer **light bundles** only
* 🧾 Keep billing simple and predictable

**Expected impact**: Stable base
👉 Don’t disrupt

---

## 6️⃣ Persona-to-Action Mapping (Summary)

| Persona                  | Churn Risk   | Best Action           |
| ------------------------ | ------------ | --------------------- |
| Price-Sensitive Newcomer | 🔴 Very High | Contract + discount   |
| Unprotected Digital User | 🟠 High      | Add-on bundling       |
| Transaction Payer        | 🟠 Medium    | Autopay incentive     |
| Loyal Long-Term          | 🟢 Low       | Rewards & referrals   |
| Minimalist               | 🟢 Low       | Stability over growth |
